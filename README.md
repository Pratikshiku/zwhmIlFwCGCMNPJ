## Hanfu Culture Promotion Website

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/45dccfbfb9e24a76bc7ca7a92577776c/ghb20250918) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (any of the listed software can be used)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Functional Description
The system is divided into (roles): regular users, administrators.

**Regular User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Homepage
4. Products
5. Image & Text Dynamic Area
6. Video Dynamic Area
7. Recent Activities
8. Hanfu Communication
9. Hanfu Knowledge
10. Personal Center
11. My Posts
12. My Orders
13. My Addresses
14. My Favorites
15. Shopping Cart
16. Change Password
17. Personal Information
18. Video Dynamic Area Management
19. Video Dynamic Area Management
20. Activity Registration Management
21. Club Management
22. Activity Ticket Management


**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center
3. Product Management
4. User Management
5. Image & Text Dynamic Area Management
6. Video Dynamic Area Management
7. Recent Activity Management
8. Activity Registration Management
9. Club Management
10. Activity Ticket Management
11. Hanfu Communication
12. Carousel Management
13. Hanfu Knowledge
14. Order Management
